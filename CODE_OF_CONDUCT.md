# Code of Conduct

We collaborate with technical rigor and respect.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate clearly and directly.
- Keep reviews specific, actionable, and respectful.
- Disclose uncertainty and assumptions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or threats
- Personal attacks and repeated bad-faith behavior
- Deliberate disruption of contributors' work

## Reporting and enforcement

Report to maintainers through private channels. Maintainers may moderate, restrict, or remove access based on severity.
