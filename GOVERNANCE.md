# Governance

## Structure

- Maintainers own merge and release decisions.
- Contributors drive issue-first development with verification notes.

## Decision process

- Prefer decisions in issue/PR threads with explicit tradeoffs.
- Escalate to short RFC when scope affects architecture or compatibility.
- Require two-maintainer sign-off for breaking workflow changes.

## Collaboration posture

Infrastructure-first, testable changes, and clear rollback paths.

## Authority boundary

Repository governance is operational and technical only; this is not a legal foundation entity.
